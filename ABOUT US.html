About the University

Barkatullah University, established in 1970 in Bhopal, is one of the premier institutions in Madhya Pradesh. The university is named after Prof. Barkatullah, a great freedom fighter and revolutionary.

The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines, including science, arts, commerce, management, law, and technology. With a strong emphasis on interdisciplinary learning and research, it serves as a hub of academic excellence in Central India.

It caters to the needs of thousands of students and strives to provide high-quality education through its affiliated colleges and departments.
