Self Study Report (SSR) for Accreditation

Welcome to the Self Study Report (SSR)

Barkatullah Vishwavidyalaya has completed its Self Study Report (SSR) for accreditation purposes. This report provides a comprehensive account of the university’s academic excellence, research output, infrastructure, and student welfare initiatives.

SSR Final

The final Self Study Report provides a complete assessment of the university's academic, administrative, and infrastructural performance.

Executive Summary

A brief overview highlighting key aspects of academic excellence, research contributions, and institutional achievements.

Evaluative Reports of the Departments

Detailed assessments of various departments, including faculty performance, student achievements, and research output.

Quantitative Metric (QnM)

Structured data covering key performance indicators such as student enrollment, faculty strength, research publications, and funding.

Extended Profile of the University

Additional details, including faculty-student ratio, institutional collaborations, and outreach programs.

Quality Indicator Framework (QIF)

Evaluation criteria covering teaching quality, research performance, governance, and student satisfaction.

Accreditation Criteria

A breakdown of the university’s performance under the accreditation framework, covering all essential quality benchmarks.
